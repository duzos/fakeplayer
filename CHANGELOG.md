# v2.1.0

- Fake players can now be given jobs (#32). Open a fake's management menu, hit the new AI button, bond the fake to you, and pick what it should do.
- The AI menu hands you marker items to tell a fake where to work (#27). Right-click to drop a waypoint or pick a chest; for a work area, right-click two opposite corners to box off a region. Markers are temporary helpers - they vanish once you wander too far from the fake, leave them sitting unused, die, log out, or sneak right-click the air to cancel (#28, #29).
- While you hold a marker you get a live preview in the world (#30): the work region shows as a coloured box you can see before you commit it, and waypoint and chest markers highlight the block you are aiming at.
- Follow (#31). Set a fake to follow and it tags along, staying close and teleporting to catch up if it falls too far behind.
- Miner (#33). Give it a region and a deposit chest and it quarries the area and banks the haul. A filter box, shown only for the miner, lets you choose what it keeps; it keeps ores by default.
- Lumberjack (#35, #36). Give it a region and it fells whole trees, scoops up the drops, and replants saplings from its own inventory. If it is carrying bonemeal it uses some to speed the new trees along.
- Courier (#37). Point it at a source chest and a deposit chest and it shuttles items between them. A filter lets you limit it to a single item or item type.
- Guard (#38). It patrols the points you mark and fights off hostile mobs that get too close. While holding the Waypoint marker in guard mode you can edit the route: right-click to add a point, sneak right-click to remove one.
- Fixed a crash when picking skins, and trending skins failing to download (#41, #42).
