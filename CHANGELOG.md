# v2.1.0

- AI sub-menu in the management gui (#27). The AI button on the AI/SL/TG row now opens a sub-screen with bond/unbond, a no-ai toggle, a job picker, and waypoint/region/deposit marker buttons. Each marker hands you a single-use AI marker item; right-click a block (or a chest, for deposit) to set the param. Region needs two clicks for corners A and B. State lives in an `aiState` compound on the entity and syncs to tracking clients.
- Session item framework (#28). The marker handed out by the AI sub-menu now carries fakePlayerUUID, ownerUUID, purpose and expiresAt on its stack. A server tick sweep silently removes the marker when it strays past 32 blocks from its bound fake, after 90 seconds of no clicks, on death/disconnect, or when you sneak right-click air to cancel.
- Job executor framework with idle job (#32). Server-tick state machine driving fake player jobs: a `JobExecutor` interface with `tick`/`onPause`/`onResume`/`serialize`, looked up per fake from `aiState.job`, state persisted under `aiState.jobState`. Pause hook (`setJobPaused`) ready for the follow-while-marking goal. Idle job navigates to the waypoint when set, otherwise stands still.
- Waypoint and deposit-chest session items (#29). Marker right-click now uses `HopperBlockEntity.getContainerAt` to accept any vanilla container (chest, barrel, shulker, dispenser, dropper, hopper) plus `WorldlyContainerHolder` blocks for modded inventories; invalid blocks reject with a chat message. New client world renderer draws a debug-line outline of the block under the crosshair while a marker is in hand and a faint outline of the value already committed on the bound fake.
- Region marker bbox preview (#30). Holding a region session item now draws a live bbox: corner A locks in yellow on the first click, a cyan box stretches from A to the block under your crosshair until you click corner B, and the bound fake's previously committed region renders as a faint green outline whenever you're holding the marker.
- Follow-owner goal (#31). High-priority goal that activates whenever the bonded owner is in 32-block range, the fake's job is set to follow, and the job is running. Walks to within 4 blocks of the owner, looks at them, and teleports if distance climbs past 16 (vanilla wolf logic).
- Miner job (#33). New `MinerJobExecutor` registered under `Job.MINER` runs a phase machine - SCANNING (region scan filtered by tag, default `c:ores`, sorted by distance), PATHING_TO_BLOCK, MINING (throttled to `minerMaxBlocksPerSecond`, default 2.5), CHECK_INVENTORY, RETURNING (path to deposit chest, dump non-tool/non-cobble/non-food, swap up to a better pickaxe). Bails on Y below `minerBailY` (default -58), on no-region/no-deposit, on 3 consecutive pathing failures, or on empty region. Lava-adjacent target gets a cobble dropped on the lava neighbour first; refuses to mine the block under its feet. Both safety rules toggleable in players.json (`minerLavaCobbleSafety`, `minerNeverMineBlockUnderFeet`).
- Guard job (#38). New job that patrols between two or more waypoints and engages hostile mobs in a configurable radius (default 12 blocks, `guardRadius` in players.json). Uses the existing `MeleeAttackGoal` and `HurtByTargetGoal`; `GuardJobExecutor` sets the target server-tick when an `Enemy` mob enters the guard's radius and resumes patrol after combat. AI sub-menu shows a Patrol row with point count and a Clear button when the job is set to Guard, and the existing Waypoint marker becomes additive in guard mode.
- Fix session item handout kicking 1.21.5+ clients (#43). `AIMarkerItem.clearAllFor` and `SessionItemSweeper` now iterate the 36 main slots plus the 5 menu-visible equipment slots (head/chest/legs/feet/offhand) instead of `Inventory.getContainerSize()`, which on 1.21.5+ includes sparse equipment-map indices (mainhand alias, body, saddle) past slot 41 and triggered an `IndexOutOfBoundsException` in `ClientboundContainerSetSlotPacket` on the client.
- Lumberjack job (#35). New job picks the nearest `#minecraft:logs` block in the configured region, paths to it, breaks it, then BFS-fells connected logs upward (capped at 64 to keep jungle trees from spiking the tick). Drops are picked up by the existing `MoveTowardsItemsGoal`. Replant is out of scope (#36).
- Courier job (#37). New `Courier` job loops between a source chest and the deposit chest, pulling matching items until full or the source is empty, then dumping them. AI sub-menu adds a Source chest button alongside Deposit when the courier job is selected; both reuse the chest-picker session item, tagged with which slot the click should write to. Filter compound on `aiState` accepts a `Tag` (item tag id) or `Item` (item id), defaults to everything.
