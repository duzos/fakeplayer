# v2.1.1

- New Fisherman job (#48). Give a bonded fake a fishing rod, set a waypoint at the water's edge and a deposit chest, and it sits and fishes, throwing out a bobber and reeling in the catch. It banks the haul when its bag fills or the rod breaks, then grabs a fresh rod from the chest and carries on. Rod enchantments work - Lure for faster bites, Luck of the Sea for better loot.
- New Farmer job (#49). Mark a region and a chest stocked with seeds, a hoe and a water bucket; the fake tills the plot, lays down water, and plants every seed type it holds (modded crops included). It bonemeals what is still growing, harvests crops once they are ripe, replants them and banks the surplus, then sits on the chest waiting for the next harvest. It grabs a fresh hoe from the chest when one wears out.
- New Crafter job (#50). Set a crafting table, a source chest and a deposit chest, then teach the fake a recipe by example: open the new teach menu from the AI menu, drop items into the 3x3 grid to preview the result, and hit Learn. It pulls the ingredients from the source, walks to the table and lays the recipe out by hand, then banks the result in the deposit and idles when the source runs dry. Chains with the other jobs - park a Crafter on a Farmer's deposit chest and wheat turns into bread on its own.
- Working fakes now visibly open the chest or barrel they are pulling from or depositing into, so you can watch them work. The AI menu also trims itself to just the markers each job actually uses.
